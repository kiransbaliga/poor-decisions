# Poor Decisions™ — Setup Guide

Setting up your own copy is completely handled by the spreadsheet's built-in wizard.

## How to Set It Up

1. **Make a Copy**
   Go to the template link using your Google Account:
   [Spreadsheet Template URL](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1inAuj4ScTlJNdCORbK7lDWQsksBBIJRw4bG_uMpGVR8/copy)

2. **Run the Setup Wizard**
   Open your newly copied sheet. Give it a few seconds to load, and a new menu item called **Poor Decisions™** will appear next to the "Help" menu at the top.
   Click it, and select **Start Setup Wizard**.

3. **Follow the Prompts**
   The sidebar will guide you through:
   - Setting your custom currency format.
   - Deploying it securely as a Google Apps Script Web App.
   - Getting the URL you need for your iOS Shortcut.

4. **Add to iPhone**
   Go to the Poor Decisions website on your iPhone, tap **Add Shortcut**, and paste your newly created URL when the shortcut asks for it on its first run.
