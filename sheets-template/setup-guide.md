# Poor Decisions™ — Setup Guide

Setting up your own copy is completely handled by the spreadsheet's built-in wizard.

## How to Set It Up

1. **Make a Copy**
   Go to the template link using your Google Account:
   [Spreadsheet Template URL](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1inAuj4ScTlJNdCORbK7lDWQsksBBIJRw4bG_uMpGVR8/copy)

2. **Run the Setup Wizard**
   Open your newly copied sheet and wait about 5-10 seconds. A new menu item called **Poor Decisions™** will appear next to the "Help" menu at the top.
   Click it, and select **Start Setup Wizard**.
   
   ⚠️ **Menu not showing up?** If the menu doesn't appear after 10 seconds:
   - Refresh the page and wait again.
   - If it still doesn't appear, click **Extensions > Apps Script**. In the script editor, select `onOpen` from the top dropdown and click **Run**. It will ask you for authorization. Once authorized, go back to your sheet, and the setup menu will be there!

3. **Follow the Prompts**
   The sidebar will guide you through:
   - Setting your custom currency format.
   - Deploying it securely as a Google Apps Script Web App.
   - Getting the URL you need for your iOS Shortcut.

4. **Add to iPhone**
   Go to the Poor Decisions website on your iPhone and tap **Add Shortcut**. Then, edit the shortcut on your phone and replace the `<your-url-here>` placeholder with your shiny new URL!
