Get Started in 3 Steps

We use Google Apps Script as a free server linking your iPhone Shortcut to your Google Sheets database.

1

Copy the Database

Copy the "Poor Decisions" Google Sheet template to your Google Drive.

Make sure you are logged into your Google account.

Copy Google Sheet
2

Run Setup Wizard

Open your newly copied Google Sheet. Click the Poor Decisions™ menu at the top and select Start Setup Wizard.

  • Confirm your currency.
  • Deploy as Web App (Execute as: Me, Access: Anyone).
  • Copy your Web App URL.
3

Add to iPhone

Download the iOS Shortcut to your phone. Open the Shortcuts app, edit the shortcut, and paste your Web App URL into the <your-url-here> placeholder block.

Tap the button below from your iPhone.

Add Shortcut to iPhone

⚠️ This button only works on an iPhone.
